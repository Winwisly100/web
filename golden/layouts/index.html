Kickstarter + Peer-Bonding Meets Movement Organizing

Climate collapse is knocking on our door, and most of us know that something must be done to change the system that continues to destroy our home.

History has repeatedly shown that to win the biggest battles for systematic social change takes massive nonviolent civil resistance. This time it’s a matter of life or death on this planet and action is needed ASAP. Voting is now totally insufficient.

To defeat our catastrophic climate collapse and horrific species extinction trajectories will take lots more than big protests. It will take several million volunteers—though a total of only about 2% of the general public— willing to risk arrest and/or engage in massive long-lasting strikes.

This will take courage. However, without brilliant organization this seed for change is likely to go to waste.

Organizations like Sunrise Movement, Extinction Rebellion, and FridaysforFuture have given us hope in the power of people organizing. They have shown us that we CAN come together to make waves and have our voices heard. To speak on behalf of all beings and the planet.

We saw that the current momentum was not matching the urgency for change, so we created WinWisely.

WinWisely is a one stop app that allows you to easily get involved in a climate action near you! Whether it be standing on the frontline, helping someone get to the event, or lifting the vibes by throwing an afterparty, we can help you fight in the most important battle of our time.
